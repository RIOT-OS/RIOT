@defgroup    sys_iec62056 IEC62056
@ingroup     sys
@brief       Utility meter data processing according to IEC 62051

The IEC 62056 module provides support for parsing and processing utility meter
data according to the IEC 62056 family of standards. This is commonly used for
smart meter communication, including electricity, gas, water, and heat meters.
